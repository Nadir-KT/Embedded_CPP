J1939 [ISO 11898, CAN 2.0B]
The standard CAN Bus supports 11- bit or 29-bit message IDs at almost every baud rate up to 1 Mbps. In contrast, SAE J1939 is limited to a 29-bit message identifier at 250 or 500 kbps(max). This is to assure maximum reliability on J1939. But it can support messages greater than 8 data bytes.
